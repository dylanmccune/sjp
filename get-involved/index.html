Donate

We encourage you to support organizations actively working to combat homelessness. Here are some trusted organizations you can donate to:

United Way

United Way supports local solutions to improve education, financial stability, and health, including programs to prevent and address homelessness.

Visit Website

SAMHSA Homelessness Programs

The Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA) funds and supports programs to help people experiencing homelessness and mental health challenges.

Visit Website

National Coalition for the Homeless

The National Coalition for the Homeless works to prevent and end homelessness by advocating for policy change and providing education and outreach.

Visit Website

Advocate

Advocacy is a powerful tool for change. Educate yourself and others about the systemic causes of homelessness, such as redlining, and participate in local events or campaigns to raise awareness.
